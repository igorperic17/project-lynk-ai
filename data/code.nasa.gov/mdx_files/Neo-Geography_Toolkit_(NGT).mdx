  automated geodesy and stereogrammetry tools designed for processing stereo imagery
  captured from satellites (around Earth and other planets), robotic rovers, aerial
  cameras, and historical imagery, with and without accurate camera pose information.
  It produces cartographic products, including digital elevation models (DEMs), ortho-projected
  imagery, 3D models, and bundle-adjusted networks of cameras. ASP's data products
  are suitable for science analysis, mission planning, and public outreach.
