# Land-Potential Knowledge System (LandPKS)

We are developing and implementing a global, open-source, digital platform that provides people with the tools and knowledge needed to assess their land&#39;s potential to be productive, resilient, and healthy. This knowledge and the submitted crowdsourced data on soil, vegetation, and crops are open for all to utilize.
