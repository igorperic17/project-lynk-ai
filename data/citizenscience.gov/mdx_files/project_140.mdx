# 2016 National Parks BioBlitz - Indiana Dunes: Miller Woods BioBlitz

We will identify as many species as possible in all taxonomic groups within the Miller Woods unit of Indiana Dunes National Lakeshore. Miller Woods is a Black Oak Savanna, one of the rarest habitats in North America, and is located in the highly industrialized city of Gary, Indiana.
