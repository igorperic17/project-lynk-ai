# 2016 National Parks BioBlitz - Lake Mead

The 2016 BioBlitz at Lake Mead National Recreation Area will explore organisms in all taxonomic groups throughout the park, with particular emphasis on reptiles at Boulder Beach and CO River Heritage Park.
