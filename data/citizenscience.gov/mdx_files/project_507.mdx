# SAFMC FISHstory

Travel back to the docks of Daytona Beach, Florida from the 1940&#39;s through 70&#39;s through the South Atlantic Fishery Management Council&#39;s (SAFMC) pilot project FISHstory. This project will train citizen science volunteers to identify and count fish in historic fishing photos.
