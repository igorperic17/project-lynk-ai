# Mojkovac BioBlitz Pilot Training

Training and support will be provided to the local government of Mojkovac Municipality, Montenegro to organize and conduct a pilot BioBlitz in Montenegro. Participants will document the local diversity of tree, medicinal herb, and mammal species as well as the presence of invasive species. An additional focus will be on building community support for the preparation and adoption of a local Biodiversity Action Plan.
