# By the People

By the People is a crowdsourcing program that invites the public to transcribe, review, and tag Library of Congress digitized historical documents as virtual volunteers. Volunteers contribute their valuable time and knowledge to create data that will improve search, readability, accessibility, and computationality of primary sources from collections that range from letters to Abraham Lincoln to the writings of Rosa Parks.
