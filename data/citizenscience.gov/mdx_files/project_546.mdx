# EPA Sanitary Survey App for Marine and Fresh Waters

The EPA Sanitary Survey App for Marine and Fresh Waters is a mobile app used to conduct routine and annual sanitary surveys for both freshwater and marine waters. The App can be used by anyone to collect information on potential sources of fecal pollution and potential harmful algal bloom (HAB) events in local surface waters.
