# Summer Science in New England

The Summer Science in New England project, performed by the New England Aquarium through a grant form NOAA, provided scientist and/or educator guided citizen science experiences for teens across the North Eastern United States. The program was supported by trained informal science center staff who incorporated citizen science while using environmental literacy principles with teens. The program offered opportunities for all participants teens, educators, and scientists, to share findings with peers at annual regional forums.
