# Worldwide Engagement for Digitizing Biocollections (WeDigBio)

Worldwide Engagement for Digitizing Biocollections (WeDigBio) is an annual 4-day event that engages participants online and onsite in digitizing natural history collections. Onsite events are hosted by museums, herbaria, universities, and other institutions and enable face to face meetups where participants engage with local collections and scientists.
