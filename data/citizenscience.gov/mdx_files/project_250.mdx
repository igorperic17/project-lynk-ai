# Youth Forest Monitoring Program 2015

The Youth Forest Monitoring Program (YFMP) is an intensive 7-week summer internship for high school students. Participants explore forest ecology, investigate natural resource careers, improve forest health through stewardship projects, and supplement forest analysis through citizen-led forest health monitoring.
