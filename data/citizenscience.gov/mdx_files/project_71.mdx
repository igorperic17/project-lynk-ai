# Rocky Mountain National Park Summer Bird Count

Volunteers counted migratory birds on the Summer Equinox of 2014, as a proof of concept expansion to the Christmas Bird Count. The project is a collaboration between the Rocky Mountain National Park and Monteverde National Park in Costa Rica. Both parks share data to study the migratory patterns of neo-tropical birds.
