# Rocky Mountain National Park Christmas Bird Count

Rocky Mountain National Park participates in the National Audubon Society&#39;s International Christmas Bird Count each December. The Christmas Bird Count goes on every year throughout North, Central, and South America during one two-week period in December, and it is intended to be a snapshot of the variety, distribution, and, to some degree, the numbers of birds present at that time.
