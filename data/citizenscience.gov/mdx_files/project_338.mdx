# 2016 National Parks BioBlitz - Valley Forge: Wildlife Watchers Photoblitz

The 2016 BioBlitz at Valley Forge National Historical Park will kick of the Wildlife Watchers program in the park including a PhotoBlitz and photography courses as tools for observing organisms in all taxonomic groups.
