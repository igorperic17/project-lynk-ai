# 2016 National Parks BioBlitz - Whiskeytown: birds, Bees, and Butterflies BioBlitz

The 2016 BioBlitz at Whiskeytown National Recreation Area will use the park as an outdoor classroom to engage local communities in the exploration of bird, pollinating invertebrate, bat, and plant species.
