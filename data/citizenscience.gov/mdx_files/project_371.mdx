# CyanoTRACKER

CyanoTRACKER will address a significant environmental issue important to inland waters, namely, Harmful Algal Blooms or popularly referred by other names  Toxic Algae ,  Algal Bloom  ,  Red Tide ,  Cyanobacteria  and  Blue Green Algae . We are looking for citizen scientists to report Algal Blooms in their neighborhood pond/lake.
