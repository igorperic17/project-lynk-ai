# 22nd Fajardo Christmas Bird Count

Annual bird count survey is conducted in the Fajardo PR area to observe and inventory bird species as part of the National Audubon Society program.  The program is led by Research Wildlife Biologist Dr. Joseph Wunderle of the International Institute of Tropical Forestry and includes observations in the El Yunque National Forest /Luquillo Experimental Forest and surrounding areas.
