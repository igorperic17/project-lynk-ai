# 2016 National Parks BioBlitz - City of Rocks: birds and Blooms

We plan to take a snapshot of biological interactions and stages of development to address phenological questions like,  during what time is the peak of the spring bird migration and the timing of vascular plants in bloom with what species of birds (especially migrants).
